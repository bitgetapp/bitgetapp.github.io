#### **Step-by-Step Instructions**

**1.  Preparation & Security Foundation**
   Before touching any "Connect" button, ensure your personal wallet (e.g., MetaMask) is set up, secured with a strong, unique password, and your Secret Recovery Phrase is written down physically and stored offline. **Never share this phrase.** Verify you are on the official Bitget website (`www.bitget.com`) to avoid phishing sites.

**2.  Navigate to the Wallet Binding Section**
   Log into your Bitget account. On the main dashboard, find and click on "Wallet" or "Assets." Look for an option labeled "Web3 Wallet," "Bind Wallet," or "Address Management" within the deposit/withdrawal section. The exact terminology may update, but the function is central.

**3.  Initiate the Connection**
   Click on "Bind New Wallet" or "Connect Wallet." A pop-up will typically appear, asking you to choose your wallet type (e.g., MetaMask, Trust Wallet, WalletConnect). Select the one you are using.

**4.  Authorize the Link from Your Personal Wallet**
   Your personal wallet extension or app will trigger a connection request. **CRITICAL:** Carefully review the permissions. It should only request to "View your address" and possibly "Suggest transactions." It should **NOT** ask for unlimited spending approval. Confirm the connection.

**5.  Verify & Label (Optional but Crucial)**
   Once connected, your wallet address will appear in your Bitget account. Take this extra step: label it (e.g., "My Main MetaMask"). This prevents errors if you bind multiple wallets later. Some platforms may require a final confirmation click like "Complete Binding."

### Pro Tips & Critical Warnings

**💡 Pro Tip: The Maker-Taker Advantage**
In both spot and futures, being a "Maker" (adding liquidity by placing a limit order not immediately matched) always incurs a lower fee than being a "Taker" (removing liquidity with a market order). For sustainable trading, cultivate the habit of using limit orders.

**⚠️ Risk Warning: The Futures Funding Fee Trap**
Never ignore the funding rate! In volatile markets, funding fees can become extremely high. Holding a long position during a period of high positive funding can erode profits even if the price moves in your direction. Always check the funding rate history before entering a futures trade.

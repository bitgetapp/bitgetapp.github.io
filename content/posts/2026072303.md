## Understanding the Core Difference: Linear vs. Exponential

Before diving into the "how," you must understand the "what." The choice between a regular rebate and an agent rebate is a fundamental decision about your earning structure.

**Regular (Friend Referral) Rebate:** This is a straightforward model. You refer a friend using your referral link/code (like AB6688). They get a 20% discount on their trading fees forever, and you earn a commission (typically 20-40% of their trading fees) for as long as they trade. Your income is directly tied to *their individual trading volume*. It's simple, passive, but limited.

**Agent (Partner) Rebate:** This is a multi-tiered, network-building model. As an agent, you not only earn from the trading fees of users you directly refer (your Tier 1), but you also earn a smaller, cascading commission from the users *they* refer (Tier 2, and sometimes Tier 3). Your referral link becomes a powerful tool for building a community. Your earnings potential scales with the growth and activity of your entire network, not just your direct referrals.

> ⚠️ **Risk Warning**: Becoming an agent often involves stricter requirements from the platform (like minimum trading volume for your network) and carries more responsibility. It's a business-building activity, not just a passive benefit. Regulatory scrutiny on multi-level marketing (MLM) models in crypto can also vary by jurisdiction.

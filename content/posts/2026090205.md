## Beyond the Basics: The Investment Logic

Registering and trading is just mechanics. The real skill lies in strategy. Cryptocurrency is a high-volatility asset class. Your goal shouldn't be to get rich quick but to build a sustainable position. This means:

*   **Diversification:** Don't put all your capital into one coin, no matter how convincing the story.
*   **Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA):** Instead of timing the market, invest a fixed amount at regular intervals. This reduces the impact of volatility.
*   **Self-Custody for Long-Term Holds:** For significant amounts you plan to hold for years, consider moving them to a private hardware wallet. "Not your keys, not your coins."

> ⚠️ **Final Risk Warning:** The cryptocurrency market is nascent and highly speculative. Only invest money you are prepared to lose entirely. Be wary of hype, "guaranteed returns," and projects you don't understand. Continuous education is your best investment.

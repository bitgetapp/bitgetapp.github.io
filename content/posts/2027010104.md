### **2026's Top 5 KYC Hurdles & Pro Solutions**

1.  **"My Proof of Address is in my spouse's name."**
    *   **Solution:** This is a common fail point. You must provide a supporting document linking you to that address, such as a marriage certificate *along with* your spouse's utility bill. Alternatively, quickly obtain a bank statement sent to that address.

2.  **"I'm using an ID with a former address."**
    *   **Solution:** Use your current, valid Passport (which doesn't show an address) as your Primary ID. Then use a current utility bill for your Proof of Address. The names must match perfectly.

3.  **"The facial recognition keeps failing."**
    *   **Solution:** This is almost always an environment issue. Move to direct, natural light (avoid overhead lights that create shadows). Ensure a strong, stable internet connection. Remove any glasses and ensure your face is not partially shadowed.

4.  **"My account is under 'Withdrawal Suspension' after verification."**
    *   **Solution:** This typically indicates a security trigger, often related to VPN usage or login from a high-risk jurisdiction. Contact Binance Support directly via the in-app chat. Be prepared to verify your location and recent activity. Patience and clear communication are key.

5.  **"How do I upgrade or change my verified country?"**
    *   **Solution:** You cannot manually change your country if you have an existing verification. You must contact support to reset your verification level, after which you can select the new country and submit documents valid for that jurisdiction.

Before you even open the OKX app, your environment must be perfect.
*   **Lighting is King:** Use bright, diffused, and **front-facing** light. Avoid backlighting (like a window behind you) at all costs. *   **Background Matters:** Use a plain, light-colored wall. Remove any other people, busy patterns, or clutter from the frame. The AI needs to focus solely on you.

**3. Clear Browser Cache & Cookies.**
Over time, cached data and cookies can become corrupted, causing conflicts with website scripts. Clearing them gives you a fresh start.
- **Chrome/Edge/Brave:** Press `Ctrl+Shift+Delete` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+Delete` (Mac). Select "All time" as the time range and check "Cached images and files" and "Cookies and other site data." Click "Clear data."

> 💡 **Tip:** After clearing cache, perform a "hard refresh" by pressing `Ctrl+F5` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac). This bypasses the cache and loads the latest version of the site from the server.

### Step 2: The Technical Deep Dive – Bypassing Network Hurdles
If the basics don't work, the issue may be related to network restrictions, which are increasingly common in 2026.

**1. Disable VPN/Proxy Services.**
While VPNs are essential for privacy, some exchange platforms, including OKX, may block traffic from known VPN IP addresses to comply with regional regulations. Temporarily disable your VPN and try accessing the site directly.

**2. Try a Different DNS Provider.**

Right-click your connection > Properties > Select "Internet Protocol Version 4 (TCP/IPv4)" > Properties > Choose "Use the following DNS server addresses" and enter the new DNS.

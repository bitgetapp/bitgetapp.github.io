### Step 2: Navigate the Verification Tiers – From Basic to Full Unlock

Binance's verification is tiered. Your goal is to progress through them.

1.  **Basic Verification (Mandatory):**
    *   **What it requires:** Government-issued photo ID (Passport, National ID, or Driver's License). You'll need to take a live selfie.
    *   **What it unlocks:** Significantly higher daily withdrawal limits (up to 100 BTC equivalent), access to Binance Spot, Margin, and some Earn products.
    *   **Time to Complete:** 10-15 minutes if your documents are ready. Approval can be instant or take up to 48 hours.

2.  **Intermediate/Advanced Verification (Highly Recommended):**
    *   **What it requires:** Proof of address (a utility bill, bank statement less than 3 months old).
    *   **What it unlocks:** Fiat deposit and withdrawal channels (bank transfer, credit/debit card), higher P2P trading limits, access to Binance Futures and options.
    *   **Why it's critical:** This tier connects the crypto world to your traditional bank account. It's essential for cashing out profits.

### Step 3: Execute the KYC Process – A Flawless Walkthrough

Follow this ordered list for a smooth verification:

1.  **Log in** to your Binance account and click on your profile icon.
2.  **Go to [Identification](https://www.binance.com/en/my/security)** or find "Verify" in your account dashboard.
3.  **Select your country/region of residence.** This is irreversible and must match your document.
4.  **Choose your document type** and follow the on-screen prompts.
    *   Ensure your ID is valid, not expired, and all corners are visible in the photo.
    *   For the selfie, ensure good lighting, a neutral background, and no glasses or hats that obscure your face.
5.  **For address verification,** upload a clear, color scan or photo of your document. The name and address must match your submitted ID.
6.  **Submit and wait.** Do not refresh the page or submit multiple times.

    If you use Google Authenticator's new cloud sync feature, understand you are trading some convenience for a different risk profile—your seed is now stored encrypted under your Google account. Ensure your Google account itself has maximum security (strong password, 2FA).

*   **Test Regularly:** Make it a quarterly habit to test your backup. Use the secret key to set up GA on a spare old phone. If it works, your backup is valid.
*   **Disable Old 2FA:** Once GA is confirmed working, you can usually disable the less secure SMS-based 2FA from the same security settings page.

*   **⚠️ RISK WARNING #3: The False Sense of Security.**

Always use a dedicated, clean device for crypto activities.

### 1. Understanding the "Device Limit" Trap: Why Binance Says No

*   **IP Address & Network:** Registering from a public WiFi, corporate network, or a VPN/IP proxy service used by thousands raises immediate red flags.
*   **Browser/App Fingerprint:** Canvas rendering, installed fonts, screen resolution, timezone, language settings, and even your browser's unique header combination.
*   **Device Identifiers:** For apps, things like Android ID, Advertising ID, or iOS Vendor Identifier. Repeated registration attempts from the same core device ID, even after app re-installs, get flagged.
*   **Behavioral Patterns:** Click speed, form-filling rhythm, and using automated form-fillers like password managers during registration can mimic bot behavior.

**The core issue is association.** If any of these fingerprints are linked to previous flagged or banned accounts, your new registration attempt is blocked preemptively. The system isn't targeting *you*; it's targeting a pattern it deems risky.

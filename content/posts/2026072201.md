*   **Document Issues:** Blurry photos, glare on the ID, cropped edges, or expired documents are instant red flags. The system needs a crystal-clear, complete image.
*   **Information Mismatch:** The name on your ID must *exactly* match the name you used to register your Binance account (including middle names). Even a minor typo can cause failure.
*   **Selfie Problems:** The photo must be a live selfie, not a picture of a picture. Poor lighting, wearing hats or glasses that cause glare, or not fully facing the camera are common culprits.

Navigate to [Buy Crypto] -> [Bank Deposit] or [Credit/Debit Card]. For 2026 users, P2P trading remains a top choice for flexibility, but direct bank transfers (via partners like Wise or Revolut) often offer the best rates for larger amounts.

**3. Executing the Deposit**

### The Withdrawal Process: Getting Your Profits Out Safely

**1. Pre-Withdrawal Preparation**
Move the crypto you wish to withdraw (e.g., USDT) from your "Spot Wallet" to your "Funding Wallet." This is a mandatory internal transfer step many beginners miss.

**2. Choosing the Withdrawal Network**
This is the **most critical step**. Click [Withdraw] on your USDT balance. You'll see a list of networks: BEP-20 (BSC), ERC-20 (Ethereum), TRC-20 (Tron), etc.
*   **Rule:** The network you select **MUST** match the network supported by your destination wallet or exchange.

**3. Entering Destination Details**
Paste the destination wallet address. Use the "Memo/Tag" if required (common for XRP, XLM, exchanges). Double-check every character. A wrong address means lost funds forever.

**4. Confirmation & Fees**
Review the network fee (miner/gas fee). Confirm the transaction. You will receive an email to finalize the withdrawal. Withdrawals are not instant; blockchain confirmation times apply.

### **5. Common Issues and Troubleshooting**

*   **"No History Found":** Double-check your date range filters. Ensure you are looking at the correct rebate type (Referral Commission, not Trading Fee Rebate). If you are a new user or your referrals haven't traded, there will be no history.
*   **Delayed Credits:** Commissions are not credited in real-time. There can be a short delay (from a few minutes to an hour) between your referral's trade and the appearance of the commission in your history.
*   **Data Discrepancies:** If you believe a commission is missing or incorrect, the first step is to check Binance's official commission structure and rules. Commission rates can vary based on your VIP level or your referral's VIP level. If a discrepancy persists, contact Binance Support with your referral's trade details and timestamps.

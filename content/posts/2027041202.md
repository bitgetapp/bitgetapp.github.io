### The Step-by-Step Verification Process

**1.  Log In & Navigate to Verification**
After registering your account, log into the OKX app or website. Navigate to your account profile or security center. You will find a prominent section labeled "Identity Verification" or "Verify Now." Click to begin.

**2.  Select Your Correct Region**
This is **crucial for 2026**. OKX's requirements vary significantly by jurisdiction. From a dropdown menu, carefully select the country/region that matches the government-issued ID you will use. Selecting the wrong region can lead to immediate rejection or future compliance issues.

**3.  Choose Verification Level (Individual)**
OKX typically offers tiers (e.g., Basic, Intermediate, Advanced). For full spot and futures trading, you will need the highest "Advanced" or "Individual" verification. Select this option to proceed.

**4.  Enter Personal Information**
You will be prompted to enter details exactly as they appear on your official ID:
*   **Full Legal Name:** No abbreviations or nicknames.
*   **Date of Birth.**
*   **Residential Address:** Must be your current, verifiable address.

**5.  Submit Document Images**
You will need to upload photos or scans of two documents:
*   **Primary ID:** A government-issued passport, national ID card, or driver's license.
*   **Proof of Address (PoA):** A utility bill, bank statement, or tax document issued within the last 3 months, clearly showing your name and address. **Tip:** Ensure every corner of the ID is visible, and the text is perfectly legible. Blurry or cropped images are the #1 cause of delays.

**6.  Complete the Liveness Check**
The system will prompt you for a real-time selfie or short video. Follow the on-screen instructions, which may include turning your head or blinking. Ensure good, even lighting and a neutral background. Do not wear hats or glasses that obscure your face.

**7.  Submit and Wait for Review**
Once submitted, your application enters the review queue. OKX states this can take from a few minutes to several business days. You will receive an email and in-app notification upon completion.

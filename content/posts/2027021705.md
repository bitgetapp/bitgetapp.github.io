### The Expert's Corner: Insights & Warnings

**🔒 Security Deep Dive**
Beyond 2FA, use a unique, strong password for Bitget. Consider a password manager. Never share your credentials, seed phrases, or 2FA codes with anyone. Bitget (or any legitimate exchange) will never ask for them via email or chat.

**💡 Understanding Order Books & Liquidity**
The numbers and charts on the trading view represent the "order book." The "bid" is the highest price buyers want to pay. The "ask" is the lowest price sellers want. The difference is the "spread." Trading high-volume pairs (like BTC/USDT) means a tighter spread and easier order execution.

**📈 Beyond Spot: A Glimpse at Other Products**
Once comfortable, you might explore:
*   **Copy Trading:** Mirror the trades of experienced, vetted traders on the platform. This is a powerful learning tool.
*   **Futures (High Risk):** Allows betting on price direction with leverage (borrowed funds). **Extreme caution required.** Leverage magnifies both gains and losses—beginners should avoid it initially.

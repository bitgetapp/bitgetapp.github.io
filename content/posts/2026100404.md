### <span style="text-shadow: 0 0 8px #a855f7;">2. Optimization: Configure DNS and Clear Local Cache</span>

Sometimes, the blockage isn't just at the network level. Your device's DNS (Domain Name System) might be resolving `binance.com` to a blocked IP, or local cached data might be causing conflicts.

*   **Action (DNS):** Change your device's DNS settings to a public, privacy-focused DNS provider like **Cloudflare (1.1.1.1)** or **Google DNS (8.8.8.8)**. This ensures your device asks a neutral party for Binance's real address.
*   **Action (Cache):** Before attempting to log in with your new VPN connection, **clear your browser's cache and cookies** for the Binance domain. On the Binance app, use the "Clear Cache" function in the settings.

### Step-by-Step Guide to Enabling 2FA on Binance

**1. Log In & Access Security Settings**
   Log into your Binance account. Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner and select **"Security"** from the dropdown menu. This is your command center for all safety protocols.

**2. Choose Your 2FA Method**
   In the Security dashboard, locate the **"Two-Factor Authentication"** section. You will see options for **Google Authentication** and **SMS Authentication**. We recommend setting up **Google Authenticator** first, as it is more secure than SMS, which can be vulnerable to SIM-swapping attacks.

**3. Setting Up Google Authenticator (Recommended)**
   a. Click **"Enable"** next to Google Authentication.
   b. You will be prompted to download the Google Authenticator app from your phone's app store (iOS/Android) if you haven't already.
   c. In the Binance interface, you will see a QR code and a manual setup key (a string of letters/numbers). **Crucially, write down this manual key on paper and store it in a safe, offline location.** This is your backup if you lose your phone.
   d. Open the Google Authenticator app, tap "+", and scan the QR code. A 6-digit, time-sensitive code will now appear in the app.

**4. Verify and Complete Setup**
   a. Back on Binance, enter the 6-digit code from your Authenticator app.
   b. You will also be asked to enter your fund password and may receive an email confirmation. Complete these verification steps.
   c. **Success!** Google 2FA is now active. Every time you log in or perform sensitive actions (withdrawals, API management), you will need to enter a fresh code from the app.

**5. (Optional) Setting Up SMS Authentication**
   While less secure, SMS 2FA can act as a secondary backup or for specific verifications.
   a. In the same "Two-Factor Authentication" section, click **"Enable"** next to SMS Authentication.
   b. Ensure your phone number is verified in your account.
   c. You will receive a 6-digit code via SMS. Enter it to activate.

### Pro Tips & Critical Risk Warnings

**🛡️ Pro-Tip: The 1-2 Punch**
For maximum security, enable **both** Google Authenticator and SMS 2FA. Set Google Auth as your primary method for logins and withdrawals. Use SMS as a secondary verification for certain actions or as a recovery pathway. This layered approach creates significant hurdles for any attacker.

**⚠️ RISK WARNING #1: The Irrecoverable Backup Key**
That manual key from Step 3 is **more important than your password**. If you lose your phone and didn't save this key, recovering access to your Binance account becomes an extremely difficult, time-consuming process involving lengthy support tickets. **Write it down on paper. Do not store it in a note on your phone or computer.**

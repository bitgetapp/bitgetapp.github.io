### Critical Pitfalls to Avoid (The "Instant Reject" Triggers)

1.  **Mismatched Information:** The name on your ID, proof of address, and your registered Binance account **must be identical**. Nicknames or minor spelling differences will cause rejection.
2.  **Expired Documents:** All submitted IDs must be valid. An expired passport is an instant fail.
3.  **Digital POA Rejection:** While some digital bank statements are accepted, screenshots of account profiles or editable PDFs are often rejected. A physical scanned statement is safest.
4.  **VPN Usage:** Never use a VPN or proxy during KYC submission. Your IP location must be consistent with your country of residence and documents.

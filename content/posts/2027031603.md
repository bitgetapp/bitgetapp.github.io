*   **Bank Transfer (Most Economical for Large Sums):** Link your bank account via the provided details. Deposit your local currency (USD, EUR, etc.) into your Binance "Fiat" wallet. Once cleared, use the "Spot" trading pair (e.g., USD/BTC) to convert to crypto. This method typically has the lowest percentage fees for larger amounts.
*   **Third-Party P2P Trading (Strategic Alternative):** This is a peer-to-peer marketplace. You buy USDT or other stablecoins from a verified seller at an agreed price, paying them via their preferred method (bank transfer, PayPal, etc.). Once they confirm receipt, Binance releases the crypto to you. **Why use it?** You can often find rates better than the official card/bank buy price, especially in certain regions. It requires more diligence in selecting high-reputation, high-volume merchants.

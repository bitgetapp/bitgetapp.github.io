### Pro Tips & Critical Warnings

**Essential Security Checklist**
*   **Password:** Use a unique, strong password managed by a password manager (e.g., Bitwarden, 1Password).
*   **Device Security:** Use a dedicated device or a clean profile for trading if possible. Avoid public Wi-Fi.
*   **Email Security:** Secure your registration email with 2FA. It's your recovery lifeline.

**Common Pitfall: Network Fees**
When withdrawing crypto, the network fee (gas) is deducted from the amount sent. For example, moving $10 of ETH on the ERC-20 network might cost $5 in gas—a 50% loss. **Always check estimated fees before confirming.** Use networks like Polygon or Arbitrum for lower fees when available.

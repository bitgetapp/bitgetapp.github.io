### Step 5: Developing a Sustainable Investment Logic
Tools are useless without a strategy. Here’s a foundational framework:

*   **Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA):** Instead of timing the market, invest a fixed amount into an asset like BTC or ETH at regular intervals (e.g., weekly). This averages your entry price over time and reduces emotional decision-making.
*   **Portfolio Allocation:** Don't go "all-in" on one coin. A common strategy is a core-satellite approach: a large portion in established assets (BTC, ETH) and smaller allocations to higher-potential "satellite" altcoins.
*   **Case Study - The DCA Advantage:** Imagine you invested $100 weekly in BTC throughout 2023's volatility. While some buys were at highs ($30k+) and others at lows ($16k), your average cost basis would be significantly smoothed out, likely positioning you well for the next cycle, compared to someone who invested a lump sum at the 2023 peak.

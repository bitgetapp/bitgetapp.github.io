**2.  Accessing Account Security Settings**
*   **On Website:** Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner and select **[Dashboard]**. Navigate to **[Security]** from the left-hand menu.
*   **On App:** Tap the profile icon, then go to **[Security]**.
*   **Critical Check:** This is your account's command center. Review all enabled security features (2FA, Anti-Phishing Code, Device Management) before proceeding.

**3.  Modifying Your Email Address**
*   In the **[Security]** page, find the "Email" section and click **[Change]**.

*   After verification, enter your **new email address**. You will receive a confirmation link at the new email. **Click that link within the time limit** to finalize the change.
*   **Post-Change:** The old email will be disassociated. Your new email becomes your primary login and communication channel.

**4.  Modifying Your Mobile Number**
*   In the **[Security]** page, find the "Mobile" section and click **[Change]**.

*   Enter your new phone number. You will receive an SMS verification code. Enter it to confirm.
*   **Implication:** This changes the number for SMS-based 2FA and security alerts. Update this promptly if you change carriers or countries.

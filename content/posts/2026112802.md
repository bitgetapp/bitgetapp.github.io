### Your Step-by-Step Guide to Depositing USDT on Binance

1.  **Log In & Navigate to 'Wallet'**
    Log into your Binance account on the web platform or mobile app. On the top navigation bar (web) or the bottom menu (app), find and click on **"Wallet"**, then select **"Fiat and Spot"** or simply **"Overview"**.

2.  **Initiate the Deposit**
    Click the **"Deposit"** button. A search bar will appear. Type in **"USDT"** and select it from the list. You will now see a crucial screen with multiple options.

3.  **Choose the CORRECT Network (Most Critical Step)**
    Binance will show you a list of networks on which you can receive USDT (e.g., BNB Smart Chain (BEP20), Ethereum (ERC20), Tron (TRC20), Arbitrum, Optimism). **This choice is irreversible.** You must select the **exact same network** your sender (e.g., another exchange, your private wallet) is using to withdraw. Sending USDT on the ERC20 network to a BEP20 address will result in permanent loss.

4.  **Copy Your Deposit Address**
    Once you select the network, your unique deposit address (a long string of letters and numbers) and a QR code will be generated. **Always double-click to highlight and copy the address directly from Binance.** Never type it manually.

5.  **Confirm and Complete the Transfer**
    Go to your funding source (e.g., another exchange like Coinbase). Initiate a USDT withdrawal, paste the Binance deposit address you just copied, and select the **matching network**. Confirm all details are identical before submitting. The funds will appear in your Binance account after the required blockchain confirmations.

6.  **Verify the Deposit**
    Return to your Binance Wallet (Fiat and Spot). Your USDT balance should update. You can click on **"Transaction History"** to see the deposit status, which will show as "Completed" once fully processed.

### Pro Tips & Critical Risk Warnings

**🛡️ Network Selection Deep Dive:**
*   **For Lowest Fees & Speed:** Use **Tron (TRC20)** or **BNB Smart Chain (BEP20)**. These are ideal for transfers under $1,000.

*   **Newer, Efficient Networks:** **Arbitrum** and **Optimism** offer Ethereum compatibility with much lower fees. Gaining popularity.

<div style="padding: 16px; background-color: white;">

To deposit fiat currency (like USD, EUR) and increase withdrawal limits, you must complete "Know Your Customer" (KYC) verification. This is a standard, mandatory process on all reputable exchanges.

1.  **Navigate to Verification:** Log into your account. Go to your profile icon (usually top right) and find "Verification" or "Identity Verification" in the menu.
2.  **Select Your Country/Region:** Choose your country of residence from the list. The required documents will vary based on this selection.
3.  **Provide Personal Information:** You will need to accurately enter:
    *   First Name and Last Name (must match your ID).
    *   Date of Birth.
    *   Residential Address.
4.  **Submit ID Document:** You will be asked to upload images of a government-issued ID.
    *   **Accepted Documents:** Typically a Passport, National ID Card, or Driver's License.
    *   **Photo Guidelines:** Ensure the document is valid, all four corners are visible, the text is clear, and there is no glare. You may need to take a photo of the front and back.
5.  **Submit and Wait:** Review all information for accuracy and submit. Basic verification (Level 1) is usually approved within a few minutes to a few hours.

> ⚠️ **Risk & Compliance Tip:** Providing false information during KYC will result in your account being permanently suspended. This process exists to protect the platform and its users from illicit activities.

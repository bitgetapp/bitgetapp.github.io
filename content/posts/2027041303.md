#### **Pro Tips & Critical Risk Management**

**💡 Pro Insight:** The biggest alpha often comes from tracking wallets that are *accumulating* a token quietly over days or weeks before any major news or pump. Look for consistent, medium-sized buys, not just one massive splash.

**⚠️ RISK WARNING 1: Sniper Bots & Front-Running**
You are not alone in tracking these wallets. Sophisticated sniper bots can execute trades milliseconds after a smart money transaction is broadcast to the mempool, often before your alert even pops up. By the time you manually swap, the price may have already moved 20-30%. This is a significant execution risk.

**💡 Pro Insight:** Use the GMGN leaderboard's "Success Rate" metric over pure profit. A wallet with a 70% win rate and moderate gains is often more reliable than one with a 30% win rate and a few moon shots.

**⚠️ RISK WARNING 2: Wash Trading & Deceptive Signals**
Some entities engage in wash trading—trading with themselves to create fake volume and activity—to lure followers into a pump. Always verify if the wallet's transactions are with independent, reputable counterparties or if they're circular.

**💡 Pro Insight:** Cross-reference GMGN alerts with on-chain sentiment tools and basic fundamental analysis. Is there a genuine product, active community, or upcoming catalyst for the token being bought?

**⚠️ RISK WARNING 3: Over-Diversification & Alert Fatigue**
Tracking 100 wallets will generate overwhelming noise and lead to "alert fatigue," causing you to miss the truly important signals. Start with 5-10 high-conviction wallets and master following them first.

The foundation of a smooth verification is preparation. Binance accepts several primary documents, but your choice matters for speed and success rate.

1.  **The Gold Standard: Passport.** This is the most universally accepted and recommended document. Ensure it's valid for at least 6 months. The machine-readable zone (MRZ) at the bottom makes it easy for automated systems to read.

**Pro-Tip for 2026:** With increasing AI-powered checks, ensure your document is:
*   **Uncropped & Full-Frame:** Capture all four corners.
*   **High-Resolution & Well-Lit:** No glare, shadows, or blur. Use natural light if possible.
*   **Physically Present:** Screenshots or scans of scans are often rejected. Place the physical document on a dark, non-reflective surface.

> ⚠️ **Risk Alert:** Never edit your ID document (e.g., Photoshop out a blemish). This can be flagged as fraud and lead to an immediate and permanent ban. Authenticity is non-negotiable.

*   **Face:** Ensure your full face is visible, without hats, sunglasses, or heavy filters. A neutral expression works best.

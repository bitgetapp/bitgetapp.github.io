### The Step-by-Step Modification Process

**1.  Access Your Personal Center & Initiate**
   *   Log into your Binance account via the website or app. **Pro Tip:** Use the website for this process; the larger screen makes navigating documents easier.
   *   Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner and select **[Identification]** or **[Account]** from the dropdown menu.
   *   Here, you will see your current verification level and personal details. Look for an option labeled **"Personal Details," "Account Information,"** or similar. Do not click "Verify" if you are already verified; you need the "Edit" function.

**2.  Locate the Specific Field to Edit**
   *   The interface will display your registered information: Full Name (as per ID), Date of Birth, Nationality, and Residential Address.
   *   **Critical:** Not all fields are editable after verification. Typically, you can update your **phone number, residential address, and postal code** without a full re-verification. Changing your **legal name or date of birth** is highly restricted and often requires submitting a support ticket with legal documents (e.g., marriage certificate, court order).
   *   Click the **"Edit"** or pencil icon next to the field you need to change.

**3.  Submit Supporting Documentation**
   *   For an address change: You will be prompted to upload a **proof of residence**. This must be an official document issued within the last 3 months (e.g., bank statement, utility bill, government-issued tax document) that clearly shows your new address and your name.
   *   **Format is Key:** Upload clear, color scans or photos. All four corners of the document should be visible. The text must be legible. PDF or JPG/PNG formats are accepted.
   *   Follow the on-screen instructions to upload the file securely.

**4.  Review and Confirm the Changes**
   *   A confirmation screen will summarize the change you are requesting. **Double-check every character.**
   *   You may receive a 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) code via your authenticator app or email to authorize this sensitive change. **Never share this code.**
   *   Click **"Submit"** or **"Confirm."** You will see a notification that your request has been received.

**5.  Await Processing and Verification**
   *   Processing times vary. For simple updates like an address, it can take from **a few hours to 3 business days**. More complex changes (like name) can take weeks.
   *   Do not submit multiple tickets for the same issue, as this will delay the process. You can check the status under **[Application History]** or **[Support]** in your account center.
   *   Once approved, you will receive a confirmation email. Your account will reflect the new information.
